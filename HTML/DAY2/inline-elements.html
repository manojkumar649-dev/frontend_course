ms dhoni ipl entry

In the Indian Premier League IPL, Dhoni plays for Chennai Super Kings (CSK), leading them to the fin on ten occasions and winning it five times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021 and 2023) jointly sharing this record with ROHITHSARMA. He has also led CSK to two Champions League T20 titles in 2010 and 2014. Dhoni is among the few batsmen to have scored more than five thousand runs in the IPL, as well as being the first wicket-keeper.
